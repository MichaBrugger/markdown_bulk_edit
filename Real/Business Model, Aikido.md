# Business Model, Aikido

Aikido is a Japanese martial art in which the strength of an attacker is used against him. As a business model, Aikido allows a company to offer something diametrically opposed to the image and mind-set of the [[Competitor|competition]]. This new [[Customer Value Proposition|value proposition]] attracts [[Customer|customers]] who prefer ideas or concepts [[Culture, Hipster|opposed to the mainstream]].[^1]
