# Business Model, Revenue Sharing

Revenue Sharing refers to firmsâ€™ practice of sharing revenues with their [[Stakeholder|stakeholders]], such as [[Complementor|complementors]] or even rivals. Thus, in this business model, advantageous properties are merged to create [[Symbiotic Effect|symbiotic effects]] in which additional [[Profit, Sharing|profits are shared]] with partners participating in the extended [[Value, Creation|value creation]].
