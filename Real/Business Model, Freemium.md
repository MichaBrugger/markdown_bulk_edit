# Business Model, Freemium

The basic version of an [[Customer Value Proposition|company offering]] is given away for [[Price, Free|free]], with the hope of eventually persuading the [[Customer|customers]] to pay for the premium version at a later date. The free offering is able to [[Business, Growth|attract the highest number of customers possible]] for the company. The generally smaller number of paying, [[premium]] customers [[Revenue, Generation|generate the revenue]], which also [[Revenue, Cross-Financing|cross-finances]] the free offering.[^1]
